About

PhD in 2008, at Karnatak University, Dharwad, India, in the field of Analytical Chemistry. Later Joined Apotex Pharmachem India Pvt Ltd., Bangalore, a pharma company and worked in A R&D section for 3 years. Later went to South Korea in 2012 for Postdoc studies at POSTECH, for one year. And then to IBD, UTM, Malaysia for another Postdoc. Worked as Assistant Professor in Chemistry, MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore. Currently working as Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemistry, Davangere University, Davangere.
